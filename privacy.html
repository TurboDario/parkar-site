Privacy Policy

This policy explains how ParKar handles your data. ParKar does not require an account.

Data we process

  • Approximate/precise location: only when you choose to save where you parked.
  • Device data: Android version and non‑personal identifiers for anonymous analytics.

Purpose

  • Save and show your parking location.
  • Improve the app in aggregate.

Retention

Parking locations are stored locally on your device and you can delete them anytime.

Third parties

If you use maps or navigation, Google Maps may process data under its own terms.

Contact

Support: support form or support@turbomonguer.dev.
